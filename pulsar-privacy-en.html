Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

PULSAR ("the App") is developed by OrionWavesStudio. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We comply with the GDPR and applicable data protection laws.

2. Data We Collect

Anonymous identifier: The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign a unique anonymous identifier (UID) to each user. This identifier is not linked to any personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

Game data: Your scores, combo history, AI archetype, Mastery stage, and session data are stored locally on your device (Room database + DataStore) and may optionally be synchronized to Firebase Firestore for the global leaderboard.

Technical information: Standard analytics data such as device model, OS version, and app version may be collected through Firebase Analytics to improve performance and stability.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We never collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information. No account creation is required to use PULSAR.

4. How We Use Data

Your anonymous UID and game data are used only to display your score on the global leaderboard, restore your progress after reinstalling the app, and power the AI engine that adapts the difficulty to your play style.

5. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google LLC): Used for anonymous authentication, Firestore database storage, and Analytics. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Google Play Billing: Used to process the one-time Premium purchase (€2.99). Payment data is handled exclusively by Google and is never accessible to OrionWavesStudio.

6. Data Retention

Your anonymous game data is retained for as long as your Firebase anonymous account exists. You may request deletion by contacting us (see Section 9). Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since all data is anonymous, we cannot identify you personally — but we can delete all data associated with your anonymous UID upon request.

8. Children's Privacy

PULSAR is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will inform you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
